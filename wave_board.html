CUSTOM WAVE BOARDS

ARCHITECTURAL WAVE PANELS

Transform flat surfaces into dynamic architectural features with our premium Wave Boards. Using advanced 3-axis CNC machining, we sculpt intricate, flowing textures onto MDF, WPC, Plywood, and Acrylic sheets. These panels create a stunning interplay of light and shadow, adding depth and movement to any interior space.

Ideal for wall cladding, reception backdrops, and furniture facades, our wave boards are available in over 50+ standard patterns or fully customized designs. We ensure seamless continuity across panels, allowing for large-scale installations without visible breaks in the pattern flow.
