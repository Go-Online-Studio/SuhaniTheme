The Future of 5-Axis CNC Precision

Jan 30, 2026 Tech Team 5 Min Read

Multi-axis machining is revolutionizing wood carving and metal fabrication. Learn how Suhani Industries uses 5-Axis technology to deliver intricate designs with zero error margins.

1. What is 5-Axis Machining?

Unlike traditional 3-axis machines that move on X, Y, and Z axes, 5-axis machines add two rotary axes (A and B). This allows the cutting tool to approach the workpiece from any direction, enabling the creation of complex geometries and undercuts that were previously impossible.

2. Benefits for Architectural Projects

  • Speed: Complex parts can be machined in a single setup, reducing fixture time.
  • Precision: Fewer manual movements mean higher accuracy (tolerance up to 0.01mm).
  • Surface Finish: The tool creates smoother curves, reducing the need for manual sanding.
